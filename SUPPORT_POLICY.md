## What support covers

Support is provided for:

- **Access issues** — "I can't download my purchase"
- **Product questions** — "I don't understand step 3 in the buyer instructions"
- **Technical clarifications** — "This code is giving me an error, what do I do?"
- **Update notifications** — "How do I get the new version?"
- **Billing questions** — "I was charged incorrectly" (redirect to Gumroad if needed)

Support does **not** cover:
- Custom development work (e.g., "Can you build this for me?")
- Consulting services beyond what the product includes
- Questions about topics not related to the purchased product

If you need custom work, email a.gharami.325@westcliff.edu with "Custom Work Request" in the subject line.

## How to write a good support email

To get the fastest possible response, include:

1. The product name you purchased
2. What you were trying to do
3. What happened (the specific error or confusing step)
4. Your operating system or environment (if technical question)

**Example:**
> Subject: Question about Firebase SaaS Starter Kit
>
> Hi Arun,
> I purchased the Firebase SaaS Starter Kit yesterday. I'm trying to follow Step 3 in the deployment checklist, but when I run `firebase deploy` I get a "permission denied" error. I'm on macOS with Firebase CLI v13. What should I check?
