## New Recommended Flagship Add-On Product

### Product Name

**Lead.AI Client Acquisition Command Center**

### Short Positioning

A practical system for finding local businesses, auditing their AI automation opportunities, writing personalized outreach, and turning conversations into paid AI chatbot / automation projects.

### Why This Product Fits the Market

Small businesses want AI help but often do not know what to automate first. Freelancers want AI income but struggle to find customers. This product connects both sides.

The product should sell the outcome clearly:

> Find better prospects. Write better outreach. Pitch AI automation with confidence. Turn one local business problem into one paid project.

## Non-Negotiable Quality Rules

1. Every product must solve one painful buyer problem.
2. Every Gumroad page must show what the buyer gets in the first 30 minutes.
3. Avoid generic “AI prompts” language. Sell workflows, outcomes, templates, and real use cases.
4. Add preview samples to build trust.
5. Add screenshots or mockups for every product.
6. Keep claims honest. Do not promise income.
7. Create a receipt message that tells the buyer exactly what to open first.
8. Use one strong CTA: “Start with the 7-day action plan.”
