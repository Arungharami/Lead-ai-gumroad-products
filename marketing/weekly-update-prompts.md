# Weekly Update Prompts — Lead.AI

**Use these prompts to generate weekly update emails for your Gumroad buyer list.**  
**Frequency:** Every 1–2 weeks. Not every week if you have nothing useful to say.

---

## Prompt 1: Product improvement update

```
Write a short weekly update email about an improvement I made to [PRODUCT NAME].

What improved: [SPECIFIC CHANGE]
Why I made it: [REASON — buyer feedback, something I noticed, etc.]

Requirements:
- Subject: specific about what improved
- Under 150 words
- Personal tone — "I updated X because [real reason]"
- Re-download instructions if relevant
- One question at the end: "Has this come up for you?" or "Let me know if this helps"
- Not a sales email — just a builder keeping buyers informed
```

---

## Prompt 2: New product or bundle announcement update

```
Write a buyer update email announcing [NEW PRODUCT/BUNDLE NAME].

Audience: existing buyers of [EXISTING PRODUCT(S)]
New product: [WHAT IT IS]
Why they might care: [HOW IT BUILDS ON WHAT THEY ALREADY HAVE]
Price: [PRICE]
Link: [LINK]

Requirements:
- Subject: honest about the new product
- Under 200 words
- Acknowledge they are existing buyers — not just a blast to everyone
- Why this is relevant to them specifically (connects to their existing purchase)
- If relevant: mention existing buyer discount path (email to arrange)
- No fake urgency
```

---

## Prompt 3: Value email (free insight)

```
Write a weekly value email sharing one practical insight with [LEAD.AI AUDIENCE — 
AI students, freelancers, founders].

Topic: [ONE SPECIFIC INSIGHT, e.g., "how to structure an AI project for a freelance 
client" or "3 prompts that work better with Claude than ChatGPT"]

Requirements:
- Subject: the insight in one line (specific, not "Weekly tips!")
- Under 200 words
- One useful, concrete insight — not vague advice
- No product pitch unless it is truly the natural next step for the insight
- Personal tone — builder sharing what they know
- End with one question or invitation to reply
```

---

## Prompt 4: Milestone/transparency update

```
Write a short update email sharing a milestone or learning from running Lead.AI.

Milestone or learning: [E.g., "just passed 10 total sales", "updated 3 products 
based on buyer feedback", "added a new bonus to the bundle"]

Requirements:
- Honest — real numbers, real feedback, no inflation
- Under 200 words
- What I learned or what changed
- Why this matters to the buyer (they are part of what made this possible)
- No: "I am so grateful for your support" generic language
- Personal, direct, specific
```

---

## Update email rules

- Do not send a weekly update unless there is something genuinely useful to say
- Every email must have standalone value — even if the reader never buys anything else
- Plain text emails work better than HTML for personal brands
- Always reply personally to any reply you receive
- Track: what subject lines get opened? What topics get replies?
