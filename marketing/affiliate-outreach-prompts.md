# Affiliate Outreach Prompts — Lead.AI

**Context:** Gumroad has a built-in affiliate/referral system. You can invite others to refer your products for a revenue share. This file contains prompts for reaching out to potential affiliates.

---

## Who makes a good Lead.AI affiliate

- AI and data science educators (YouTubers, bloggers, newsletter writers)
- Freelancing coaches who work with tech freelancers
- Developers with newsletters or communities focused on AI/ML
- Career coaches for data scientists and AI engineers
- SaaS-focused newsletter writers

**Note:** Only reach out to affiliates who have genuine audiences that match Lead.AI's target customers.

---

## Prompt 1: Initial affiliate outreach email

```
Write an affiliate partnership email to [AFFILIATE NAME/TYPE, e.g., "a data science 
YouTuber with 5,000 subscribers"].

Context:
- My name: Arun Kumar Gharami
- My brand: Lead.AI
- Products: [LIST 1–2 most relevant products]
- What I am proposing: a Gumroad affiliate arrangement (they share a link, 
  earn a % of each sale they drive)
- What I would offer as commission: [YOUR COMMISSION RATE — e.g., 30%]

Requirements:
- Under 200 words
- Lead with why this is relevant to their specific audience (not just "I have products")
- Specific about what the products are and who they are for
- Clear about the commission arrangement
- No fake social proof ("hundreds of students love this")
- Professional, respectful, not desperate
- Easy for them to say no — no pressure tactics
```

---

## Prompt 2: Follow-up email (if no response after 7 days)

```
Write a one-paragraph follow-up to an affiliate outreach email that did not get a response.

Context:
- First email sent 7 days ago about a potential affiliate arrangement
- I am following up once — if no response to this, I will not contact again

Requirements:
- Under 80 words
- Friendly, brief
- Restate the value in one sentence
- Easy opt-out: "If this is not a fit, no worries at all"
- No multiple follow-ups — one follow-up only
```

---

## Prompt 3: Thank-you message after first affiliate sale

```
Write a short thank-you note to an affiliate after they drove their first sale.

Requirements:
- Under 100 words
- Genuine appreciation — specific to the fact that someone they referred actually bought
- Mention the commission amount (or that it is on its way via Gumroad)
- Invite them to share feedback about the product from their audience's perspective
- Personal, not automated
```

---

## Affiliate program setup on Gumroad

1. Go to Gumroad → Product → Affiliates
2. Enable affiliates for the product
3. Set your commission rate (30% is standard for digital products)
4. Share the affiliate signup link with potential affiliates
5. Gumroad handles tracking and payments automatically
