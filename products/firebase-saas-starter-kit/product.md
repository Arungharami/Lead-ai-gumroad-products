## What this product is

A complete Firebase SaaS starter kit that gives developers everything they need to launch an AI SaaS product without starting from scratch.

**Deliverables:**
- Firebase project configuration (Firestore, Auth, Hosting)
- Authentication: Email/password + Google OAuth
- Dashboard layout template (HTML/CSS/JS or React, depending on version)
- Stripe payment integration template (subscription billing)
- User management (sign up, sign in, profile, subscription status)
- Deployment checklist (Firebase Hosting + custom domain)
- README with setup steps

---

## Technical requirements

- Node.js 18+
- Firebase CLI
- A Firebase project (free tier works)
- A Stripe account (free to create)
- Basic HTML/CSS/JavaScript or React knowledge

---

## Target buyer

- Startup founder who wants to ship a SaaS product fast
- Developer building their first SaaS application
- Freelancer building a client SaaS product
- AI developer who built a model and wants to wrap it in a product

---

## What this product is NOT

- Not a production-ready enterprise system
- Not a complete business (no business logic, no AI model included)
- Not a drag-and-drop no-code tool
