# LinkedIn Launch Post Template

This LinkedIn post template is tailored for founders, software engineers, and indie hackers announcing the launch of their new AI SaaS application. It uses a "builder-to-builder" style that focuses on shipping velocity, specific technical solutions, architectural decisions, and actual product metrics. 

---

## Customization Instructions for the Buyer

### 1. Identify Your Specific Problem
Replace `[SPECIFIC_PROBLEM]` with a painful, recognizable bottleneck in your target industry. For example:
- *Before:* "...to solve the slow, manual process of generating weekly content."
- *After:* "...to solve the fact that marketing teams spend 15 hours a week manually copy-pasting data between analytics dashboards."

### 2. Tailor Your Tech Stack
If you added specific libraries or selected a different frontend framework (such as Next.js, Tailwind CSS, or SvelteKit), list them in the "Tech Stack" section to build authority with other technical professionals on LinkedIn.

### 3. Share Factual Outcomes
Keep the core features focused on practical outcomes. Avoid using empty marketing claims or self-praising adjectives. State exactly what the user achieves and how the system supports that achievement.
