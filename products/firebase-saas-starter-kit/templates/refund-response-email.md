# Refund Response Email Template

This email template is designed to handle refund requests professionally and politely. It ensures a friction-free experience for the customer while gathering valuable feedback to improve the product. It also outlines the exact steps and timelines for their refund, helping to maintain trust and goodwill.

---

**Subject:** Refund Request Received: [YOUR_PRODUCT_NAME] - [ORDER_NUMBER]
**Preheader:** We are processing your request. Please review the refund details and timeline.

Dear [CUSTOMER_NAME],

We have received your refund request for your purchase of [YOUR_PRODUCT_NAME], order number [ORDER_NUMBER].

We want every customer to have a positive experience with our products. If [YOUR_PRODUCT_NAME] did not meet your requirements, we are happy to process this refund in accordance with our Refund Policy.

---

## 1. Refund Status and Next Steps
Our billing team has initiated the refund process for your purchase:
- **Transaction Amount:** [AMOUNT]
- **Original Payment Method:** [e.g., Credit Card ending in 1234 / PayPal]
- **Refund Date Initiated:** [INSERT_DATE]

The funds are being credited back to your original payment method. Depending on your financial institution, the credit should appear on your statement within 5 to 10 business days.

---

## 2. Changes to Your Account Access
Please note that the following changes have been made to your account:
- Your access to the [YOUR_PRODUCT_NAME] premium dashboard has been revoked.
- Your license key `[LICENSE_KEY]` is now deactivated and will no longer validate.
- If you were granted access to our private GitHub repository, your access has been removed. 
- You must delete all local copies, zip files, or deployments of the codebase that were downloaded. Continued commercial use of the codebase without an active license key violates our licensing terms.

---

## 3. We Value Your Feedback (1 Quick Question)
To help us improve [YOUR_PRODUCT_NAME] for future developers and users, we would appreciate it if you could share the primary reason for your refund. If you have a brief moment, please reply to this email and let us know:
- What specific feature or capability was missing from [YOUR_PRODUCT_NAME] that you required?
- Did you run into technical errors or deployment issues that we could help fix?

Your honest feedback is valuable to us and will be used directly to guide our upcoming feature roadmap and documentation updates.

If you ever decide to return, our team will be ready to help you build. We wish you success in your software development endeavors.

Sincerely,

[YOUR_NAME]  
Founder, [YOUR_COMPANY_NAME]  
[YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL]  
[YOUR_WEBSITE_URL]  

---

> [!TIP]
> **Customer Retention Best Practices:**
> - Process refunds promptly. Delays in processing refunds can lead to negative reviews or costly chargeback disputes.
> - Keep the communication neutral and professional. A customer who receives a polite, hassle-free refund is far more likely to buy from your future product catalog than one who experiences friction.
