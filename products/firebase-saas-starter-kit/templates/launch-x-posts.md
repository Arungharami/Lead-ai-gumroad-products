## Customization Instructions for the Buyer

### 1. Maintain Factual Accuracy
Do not exaggerate your metrics. If your lighthouse score is 92, write 92. If your database queries take 60ms, write 60ms. Factual metrics build significantly higher credibility with technical buyers on X.

### 2. Embed Visual Assets in Post 4
Always attach a clean video demo (15–30 seconds) or a high-resolution screenshot to Post 4. Visual proof of a working dashboard increases engagement and click-through rates.

### 3. Keep Within the Character Limit
Ensure your customized text remains below 280 characters per post. Use concise sentences, clean line breaks, and standard spacing.
