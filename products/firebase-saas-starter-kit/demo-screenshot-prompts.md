## 5. Design Guidelines for Customizing Cover Assets

To build cohesive Gumroad assets using these generated mockups:
- **Aspect Ratios:** Always output your final covers in `16:9` dimensions (e.g., `1280x720px` or `1600x900px`).
- **Overlay Text:** Keep overlays minimal. Use bold sans-serif typefaces (e.g., Inter, SF Pro, or Roboto) to lay your product title over the generated mockups.
- **Brand Consistency:** Stick to a single color scheme (e.g., Slate and Navy Blue with Violet or Blue accents) across all covers, thumbnails, and detail graphics to establish brand consistency.
