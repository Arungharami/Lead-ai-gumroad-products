## 2. Billing and Payment Processing

We do not directly store or process your credit card, debit card, or banking details on our servers. All financial transactions are handled securely by our third-party payment processors.

- **Stripe:** Payments processed via Stripe are governed by the Stripe Privacy Policy. Stripe collects your billing details, card number, expiration date, and billing address.
- **Gumroad:** Purchases completed via Gumroad are subject to the Gumroad Privacy Policy. Gumroad collects customer contact details and transaction history.

We receive transaction metadata from these processors, including transaction status, subscription plan details, product purchase IDs, expiration dates, and the last four digits of the payment card. This data is stored in our database for account provisioning and management.
