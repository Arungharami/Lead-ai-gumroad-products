## Your first 10 minutes

**Goal: Get oriented and identify your anchor problem.**

1. Open the PDF
2. Skim the table of contents (2 minutes)
3. Read the introduction (3 minutes)
4. On a blank piece of paper or in a notes app, write down:
   - One project, client, or business problem where you think AI could help
   - One question you have about AI that you have never had clearly answered

Keep these two things in mind as you read. They will make every section feel relevant instead of abstract.

---

## Your first 1 hour

**Goal: Complete Part 1 — Explainable AI.**

1. Read Part 1 start to finish (approximately 30–40 minutes)
2. At the end of Part 1, complete the action checklist (10 minutes)
3. Look at the anchor problem you wrote down. Does XAI apply to it? If yes, note how.

After one hour you will have a working understanding of what explainable AI is, why it matters for businesses, and how SHAP and LIME work at a conceptual level.

---

## Your first day

**Goal: Complete Parts 2 and 3 and identify one concrete action.**

1. Read Part 2 — Fraud Detection (approximately 30–40 minutes)
2. Complete the Part 2 action checklist
3. Read Part 3 — Business Automation (approximately 30–40 minutes)
4. Complete the Part 3 action checklist
5. Pick one action from any checklist that you can apply to your real project or business this week
6. Write it down and set a reminder to do it

By the end of day one, you will have a clear picture of how all three domains connect, and one specific action to take in your own context.
