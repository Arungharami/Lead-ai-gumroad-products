## What this product is

A practical, jargon-free guide for people who want to understand and apply AI in real business settings. Covers three high-value AI domains:

1. **Explainable AI (XAI):** What it is, why it matters, how to use SHAP and LIME to explain model decisions
2. **Fraud Detection:** How ML-based fraud systems work, what data they need, how to evaluate them
3. **Business Automation:** How to identify automation opportunities and apply AI workflows to common business tasks

## Format

- PDF guide (primary deliverable)
- Practical examples and case studies
- Action checklists at the end of each section
- No advanced math required — concepts explained with plain language and visuals

## Target buyer

- An AI student who wants more than textbook theory
- A freelancer who wants to explain AI systems to clients
- A small business owner who wants to understand what AI can do for them
- A startup founder evaluating whether to build AI features

---

## What this product is NOT

- Not a code-heavy technical tutorial (the Fraud Detection XAI Kit is for that)
- Not a research paper
- Not a get-rich-quick scheme
