Initial release. Three-part guide covering XAI, Fraud Detection, and Business Automation with action checklists.

## v1.2.0 (planned)

**Trigger:** Release approximately 6 months after v1.1.0.

## v2.0.0 (future)

**Full revision:**
- Add 5 real mini-case studies (anonymized or fictional but realistic)
- Add interactive checklist appendix
- Add "AI Product Ideas" section: 10 AI automation products you can build and sell
- New price target: $15 (after v2.0 justifies higher value)

**Trigger:** When the product has enough buyer feedback to validate the direction.

---

## What will never change

- The core three-part structure (XAI, Fraud Detection, Business Automation)
- The plain-English, non-academic voice
- The action-checklist format at the end of each section
- Price stays at or below $15 to keep this accessible as an entry-level product
