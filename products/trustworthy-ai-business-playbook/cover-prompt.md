# Cover Image Prompt — Trustworthy AI Business Playbook

**Use this prompt in Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Adobe Firefly, or similar tools.**

---

## Primary cover prompt

```
Professional digital product cover image for an AI business guide.
Dark navy blue background with subtle geometric network nodes and connecting lines
suggesting a neural network. In the center, a clean modern book or document icon
with glowing edges in electric blue (#0099FF). Text overlay in bold white sans-serif:
"Trustworthy AI Business Playbook" on top line, smaller subtitle below:
"XAI · Fraud Detection · Business Automation". Bottom right corner: "Lead.AI" logo
in white with a small circuit-board accent. Style: modern tech, clean, professional,
minimal. Dimensions: 1600 x 900 pixels. No gradients that look cheap. No stock photo
people. No robot cartoon imagery.
```

---

## Alternative prompt (lighter version)

```
Clean product cover with a white-to-light-blue gradient background.
Abstract AI visualization: interconnected nodes forming a brain-like network
in shades of blue and teal. Large headline text "Trustworthy AI Business Playbook"
in dark navy blue, bold, left-aligned. Subtitle: "Explainable AI · Fraud Detection ·
Business Automation". Lead.AI brand mark in bottom left corner in dark navy.
Professional, clean, minimal. Like a premium O'Reilly book cover but modern.
1600 x 900 pixels.
```

## What to avoid

- Cartoon robot imagery
- Cheap gradient backgrounds
- Stock photo of people at computers
- Clip art brain images
- Overly complex, cluttered layouts
- Comic Sans or decorative fonts
