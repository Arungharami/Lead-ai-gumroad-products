**Q: The prompt gave me a generic output. What do I do?**

The most common reason for generic outputs is not replacing the placeholders with specific details. Instead of `[YOUR PRODUCT]`, write your actual product name. Instead of `[TARGET CUSTOMER]`, write a specific description like "freelance copywriters who charge $50-150/hour". Specificity is the key.
