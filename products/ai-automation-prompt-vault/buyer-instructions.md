## Step 3: Try your first prompt in 5 minutes

1. Open ChatGPT (https://chat.openai.com) or Claude (https://claude.ai)
2. Open the PDF and find prompt #1 from your starred list
3. Copy the prompt text
4. Replace the [BRACKETED] placeholder text with your specific details
   - Example: Replace `[YOUR PRODUCT NAME]` with your actual product name
   - Example: Replace `[TARGET CUSTOMER]` with your specific customer type
5. Paste into ChatGPT or Claude and hit Enter

**Tip:** The more specific you make the placeholders, the better the output.
