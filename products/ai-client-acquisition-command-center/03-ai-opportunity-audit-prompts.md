# 03 — AI Opportunity Audit Prompts

Use these prompts in ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini to automatically audit a local business's public assets, identify high-value lead capture leaks, and write custom recommendations you can copy and paste into your outreach emails.

---

## Prompt 1: The Website Lead Capture & Support Leak Scanner

Use this prompt to scan a prospect's landing page content and identify exact friction points.

### The System Prompt to Copy

```text
Act as an expert local business automation consultant and conversion rate optimization (CRO) auditor. 
I will provide you with the copy, business details, and public landing page elements of a local business website.

Analyze their page and output an audit report detailing:
1. **Critical Lead Capture Gaps**: (e.g. static contact forms, lack of evening contact options, complex intake steps).
2. **Repetitive Support Bottlenecks**: (e.g. questions about pricing, bookings, locations, or scheduling that require a manual phone call).
3. **Proposed AI Solution**: A simple, value-dense description of an automated customer service or booking chatbot. Show how it saves them hours and captures more evening leads.
4. **Custom Pitch Line**: Write a concise, 2-sentence value-first outreach sentence highlighting a specific leak you found and how a simple AI tool fixes it. Do not use hype or promise exact sales numbers.

Here are the business details:
- **Niche**: [Enter Niche, e.g. HVAC Contractor]
- **Company Name**: [Enter Name, e.g. Miller Heating & Cooling]
- **Website Page Copy / Observations**: [Paste their homepage text or describe what elements are present, e.g. has a basic contact form with 8 inputs, no chat support, phone number only active 8 AM - 5 PM]
```

## Prompt 2: The Google Review Gap Analyzer

Use this prompt to audit their public Google Business Profile or Yelp reviews to find recurring customer support complaints.

### The System Prompt to Copy

```text
Act as a customer success auditor. I will provide you with a list of recent 2-star, 3-star, or 4-star reviews for a local service business.

Analyze these reviews and identify:
1. **Recurring Communication Leaks**: (e.g. "nobody answered my evening booking question", "had to wait 2 days for an appointment confirmation", "front desk was too busy to return my call").
2. **The Business Cost**: Explain the financial cost of this leak (e.g. dropping high-value customers to speedier competitors).
3. **The Automated Fix**: Detail how a simple, automated AI SMS auto-responder or evening website assistant solves this specific customer complaint.
4. **The Value Pitch**: Write a 2-sentence conversational paragraph I can use to contact the owner, mentioning how this automated setup directly addresses their customer reviews.

Here are the reviews to analyze:
[Paste reviews here]
```

## Prompt 3: The Custom Proposal Builder

Use this prompt to build a personalized business proposal outline once a prospect agrees to a call.

### The System Prompt to Copy

```text
Act as an AI business consultant. Write a professional, concise, value-based proposal outline for the following client:

- **Client Name**: [Client Company]
- **Niche**: [Niche]
- **Identified Pain Point**: [e.g. Missed emergency calls during off-hours, wasting 5 hours a week scheduling client calls]
- **Proposed Solution**: [e.g. 24/7 lead-capture chatbot with booking integration]
- **Pricing Tier**: $500 one-time setup fee + $99 monthly retainer

Structure the proposal into 4 short sections:
1. **The Opportunity**: Explain the exact lead-capture leak and its financial impact.
2. **The Automated Fix**: Detail the workflow of the proposed AI assistant in plain English (no technical jargon).
3. **Scope & Timeline**: Outline the 4 steps to deploy the system within 7 business days.
4. **The Investment**: Show the flat setup cost and the recurring support fee. Show how capturing just ONE customer per month covers the entire annual cost.
```
