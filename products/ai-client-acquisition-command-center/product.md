## What this product is

A practical client-finding and outreach system for freelancers, AI builders, students, and consultants who want to sell AI chatbot, lead capture, and automation services to local businesses. 

It helps the buyer transition from local developer/prompter to a paid automation consultant by providing a repeatable sales workflow:
1. **Prospecting Strategy**: Identifying high-margin local businesses.
2. **AI Audit framework**: Spotting specific customer service and lead capture leaks.
3. **Outreach & Scripts**: Value-first templates for cold outreach and a 15-minute diagnostic discovery call.
4. **Proposal & CRM**: Reusable pricing structures, proposals, and pipeline tracking.
