# 06 — Follow-Up Sequence

Over 70% of cold outreach responses come from the follow-up messages, not the first touch. Business owners are busy and often open your email on their phone, intend to reply later, and forget.

Use this simple, polite 3-step follow-up sequence to gently bump your thread without sounding spammy.

---

## The Follow-Up Schedule

- **First Outreach**: Day 1
- **Follow-up 1**: Day 4 (3 business days later)
- **Follow-up 2**: Day 8 (4 business days later)
- **Follow-up 3 (Break-up)**: Day 12 (4 business days later)

---

## Follow-Up 1: The Quick Bump (Day 4)

Keep this extremely short. Simply refer to the original thread.

**Subject**: Re: Quick conversion audit for [Business Name]

Hi [Owner Name],

I know you are busy managing [Company Name]. I wanted to quickly bump this to ensure it didn't get buried in your inbox. 

Did you get a chance to see the 3-minute chatbot mockup I built for your site? 

No pressure at all, just wanted to check.

Best,

[Your Name]

---

## Follow-Up 2: The Value Add (Day 8)

Provide an extra point of value or a simple statistic to build credibility.

**Subject**: Re: Quick conversion audit for [Business Name]

Hi [Owner Name],

Quick update: I recently analyzed local [Niche] booking rates and found that businesses adding a conversational scheduler captured an average of **22% more leads** from their existing website traffic.

I still have the simple design mockup I created for [Company Name] showing how to capture these after-hours bookings.

Would you be open to a quick 5-minute call this Thursday at 10 AM to take a look?

Best,

[Your Name]

---

## Follow-Up 3: The Break-Up Email (Day 12)

Close the thread politely. This often triggers a response because it removes sales pressure.

**Subject**: Closing this thread for [Business Name]

Hi [Owner Name],

I haven't heard back from you, so I'll assume that automating your website lead capture is not a priority for [Company Name] right now.

I will close out your custom chatbot mockup template and won't crowd your inbox again.

If you ever want to revisit this in the future to capture more evening leads automatically, feel free to reach out.

Thanks for your time,

[Your Name]
