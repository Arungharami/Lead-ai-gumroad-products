# 01 — Ideal Client Profile (ICP) Worksheet

Targeting the wrong businesses is the #1 reason AI freelancers fail. If you target low-margin businesses that have low transaction values, they will not have the budget to pay for AI automation, regardless of how much time it saves them.

Use this worksheet to filter your local prospects and identify high-value targets.

---

## The ICP Golden Rules

To qualify as a high-value prospect, a business should meet at least two of these three criteria:

1. **High Average Transaction Value (ATV)**: The business sells products or services worth $500+. 
   - *Example*: A dental clinic ($1,500+ per implant), an HVAC contractor ($4,000+ per installation), or a real estate agency ($10,000+ commission).
   - *Why*: If a chatbot helps them capture just **one** extra customer per month, the system pays for itself ten times over.
2. **Clear Administrative Lead Overhead**: The business receives high-volume, repetitive inquiries that take up manual staff time.
   - *Example*: Law firms booking consultations, gyms answering membership questions, or property management companies handling maintenance requests.
3. **High Ad Spend/Marketing Activity**: The business actively spends money on Google Ads, Facebook Ads, or local SEO.
   - *Why*: If they spend $1,000/mo driving traffic to their website, they are highly motivated to optimize their conversion rate and capture every single click.
