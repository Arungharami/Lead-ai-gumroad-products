# 10 — The 7-Day First-Client Action Plan

Follow this daily execution plan to go from zero leads to booking your first paid AI automation discovery call within 7 days. Do not skip days or rush the research phase. 

Outbound sales is a momentum game. Work through this list systematically.

---

## Day 1: Target Definition & CRM Setup
- **Goal**: Establish your target focus.
- **Tasks**:
  1. Open `01-ideal-client-profile-worksheet.md`. Select exactly **2 niches** with high transaction values.
  2. Create a local folder on your computer named `Outreach-Campaign` and place `09-simple-crm-tracker.csv` in it.
  3. Import this CSV into Notion, Excel, or Google Sheets so you have an active CRM grid ready to edit.

---

## Day 2: Prospect Prospecting & Data Gathering
- **Goal**: Research and identify 15 highly qualified local prospects.
- **Tasks**:
  1. Open `04-lead-research-workflow.md`. Follow the Google Maps search steps.
  2. Find **15 local companies** in your selected niches that have active sites but lack any chat widgets, automated schedulers, or interactive FAQ elements.
  3. Find the direct email address and first name of the owner, general manager, or founder for each business.
  4. Log all 15 leads inside your CRM tracker.

---

## Day 3: Opportunity Auditing
- **Goal**: Run digital lead capture audits for your 15 prospects.
- **Tasks**:
  1. Open `03-ai-opportunity-audit-prompts.md`. Copy the "Website Audit Prompt."
  2. Paste the prompt into ChatGPT or Claude along with observations about each prospect's website.
  3. Record the 2 main digital support leaks and recommended solutions identified by the AI in the "Audit Notes" column of your CRM tracker.

---

## Day 4: Campaigns Launch
- **Goal**: Draft and send your first 15 personalized value-led outreach emails.
- **Tasks**:
  1. Open `05-personalized-outreach-templates.md`. Select the Cold Email or LinkedIn template.
  2. Draft the emails for your 15 prospects. Customize each draft with the specific lead capture leaks found on Day 3.
  3. Double-check that all names, websites, and details are perfectly accurate.
  4. Send all 15 emails/DMs. Log the "Sent Date" in your CRM.

---

## Day 5: Response Monitoring & Preparation
- **Goal**: Monitor replies and prepare your discovery materials.
- **Tasks**:
  1. Monitor your email and LinkedIn inbox for replies.
  2. If a prospect replies asking for more details, reply immediately to schedule a 15-minute Zoom call.
  3. Open `07-discovery-call-script.md` and read through it twice. Get comfortable with the diagnostic questioning process.
  4. Open `08-ai-automation-proposal-template.md` so you are ready to compile a customized proposal as soon as a call is scheduled.

---

## Day 6: The Quick Bump (Follow-Up 1)
- **Goal**: Bumping prospects who haven't replied to your Day 4 message.
- **Tasks**:
  1. For any prospect who has not replied within 2 business days, open `06-follow-up-sequence.md`.
  2. Send the "Quick Bump" follow-up message.
  3. Record the "Follow-up 1 Date" in your CRM tracker.

---

## Day 7: Pipeline Analysis & Retargeting
- **Goal**: Review campaign metrics and research another batch.
- **Tasks**:
  1. Review your CRM results: How many emails were sent? How many replies did you receive?
  2. Schedule any booked meetings for the coming week.
  3. If you have received fewer than 2 replies, repeat the Day 2 research steps to find another batch of 15 prospects. Add them to your CRM and start the process for Batch 2.

*Outbound consulting is a numbers game. Sending 15 audit-backed outreach emails per week guarantees you will consistently get in front of local decision-makers and close paid deals.*
