# 05 — Value-First Outreach Templates

These outreach templates are designed to build curiosity and offer upfront value. They avoid salesy pitches and focus entirely on a specific lead leak found during your research.

Choose the template that fits your outreach channel:

---

## Template 1: Cold Email Audit Pitch

Use this template when you have a direct email address.

### Email Template

**Subject**: Quick conversion audit for [Business Name] — missed lead opportunities?

Hi [Owner Name],

I was researching local [Niche] services in [City Name] and spent some time on [Company Website]. 

You have highly rated reviews and great branding, but I noticed a specific conversion leak that is likely dropping evening prospects:

- **The Leak**: Your booking inquiry form on [Company Website] has 8 separate required fields, and your phone lines close at 5 PM.
- **The Impact**: Many evening users browsing on their phones will not fill out a long static form or leave a voicemail. They simply hit back and call a competitor who answers instantly.
- **The Fix**: Replacing or pairing that form with a simple, conversational 3-question lead-capture chatbot that captures client details and books appointments automatically 24/7.

I mocked up a simple interactive chatbot template for your business. It takes less than 3 minutes to review.

Would you be open to a quick 5-minute screen share this Thursday to see the mockup?

Best regards,

[Your Name]  
Lead.AI Automation Consultant  
[Your Email]

## Action Tips for High Open Rates

1. **Never copy-paste raw brackets**: Always fill in the bracketed placeholders with specific, real details about the prospect.
2. **Keep the tone helpful and professional**: You are acting as an auditor offering a solution, not a pushy salesperson.
3. **Keep the email under 150 words**: Owners are busy. Short, scannable emails get double the response rate of long sales letters.
4. **Offer a mockup**: Mentioning that you already built a simple visual mockup creates massive curiosity and shows you have already done the work.
