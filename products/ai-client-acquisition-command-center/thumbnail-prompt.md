# Thumbnail Image Prompt — Lead.AI Client Acquisition Command Center

**Use this prompt in Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Adobe Firefly, or similar tools.**

---

## Primary Thumbnail Prompt

```
Gumroad product thumbnail, dimensions: 600 x 400 pixels.
Sleek dark tech theme with deep navy background.
Large, centered 3D icon of a high-tech glowing radar or targeting scope in neon hot pink (#FF6B9D) and rich purple (#C850C0).
Bold, highly readable white text in the center: "AI SALES" or "CLIENTS".
Top left corner: "Lead.AI" brand mark in white.
Clean, modern, premium quality. High-contrast so it stands out in search results.
No clutter, no long paragraphs, no blurry text.
```

---

## Alternative Prompt (Clean Minimalist Version)

```
Gumroad card thumbnail. White-to-light-blue gradient background, 600 x 400 pixels.
Abstract minimal geometric lines suggesting a funnel or bullseye target.
Left-aligned large text "Find AI Clients" in bold dark navy blue.
Lead.AI logo on the right side.
Clean layout, sleek, modern design aesthetic. Extremely readable at small thumbnail sizes.
```

## Color Palette

| Element | Color | Hex |
|---------|-------|-----|
| Background | Deep navy | #0A1628 |
| Neon pink accent | Hot Pink | #FF6B9D |
| Neon purple accent | Violet | #C850C0 |
| Main text | White | #FFFFFF |
| Brand mark | White | #FFFFFF |

---

## What to Avoid

- Cheap clip art images
- Hard-to-read text or long descriptions
- Generic pictures of computers
- Cluttered backgrounds that hide the central icon
