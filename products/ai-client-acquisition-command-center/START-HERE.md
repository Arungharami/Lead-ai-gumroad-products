## 3 Core Rules for Value-First Sales

1. **Audit Before You Pitch**: Never send a cold email saying "I build chatbots, want one?". Instead, send: "I audited your website and noticed your evening contact form is missing a conversational lead capture, which means you are missing off-hour prospects. Here is exactly how to fix it."
2. **Focus on Outcomes, Not Tech**: Local business owners do not care about vector databases, LLMs, or APIs. They care about:
   - *Capturing missed calls and evening web traffic.*
   - *Reducing customer support support costs.*
   - *Saving their office staff 5–10 hours per week of manual data entry.*
3. **Listen Diagnostically**: On your discovery calls, let the client speak 70% of the time. Ask about their current bottle-necks (e.g. how they route incoming leads, how fast they respond to inquiries) before proposing any AI solution.
