# Lead.AI Client Acquisition Command Center

## Product Summary

A practical client-finding and outreach system for freelancers, AI builders, students, and small business consultants who want to sell AI chatbot, lead capture, and automation services.

This product helps the buyer move from:

> “I know AI, but I don’t know how to get customers.”

To:

> “I know who to target, what problem to look for, what message to send, and how to pitch a simple AI automation package.”

---

## Target Buyer

- Beginner AI freelancer
- Student building an AI side income
- No-code/low-code builder
- Web developer adding AI services
- Small agency owner
- Gumroad buyer who wants a real action plan after buying prompts or SaaS templates

---

## Buyer Problem

Most AI beginners have tools, prompts, and tutorials, but they do not have a repeatable sales workflow.

They struggle with:

- Which businesses to contact
- How to identify automation problems
- How to write personalized outreach
- How to explain AI services simply
- How to price a starter project
- How to follow up without sounding spammy
- How to turn a conversation into a paid setup

---

## Core Outcome

By the end of this product, the buyer should have:

1. A clear ideal client profile
2. A list of 25–50 target businesses
3. A simple AI audit for each prospect
4. Personalized outreach messages
5. A discovery call script
6. A proposal template
7. A 7-day first-client action plan
8. A simple CRM tracker
