# 07 — Diagnostic Discovery Call Script

The goal of a 15-minute discovery call is not to sell your solution. The goal is to **diagnose the business's pain points**.

If you start pitching technical features immediately, you lose the prospect. If you ask diagnostic questions and uncover how much money they lose from missed leads, the sale happens naturally.

---

## Phase 1: Set the Agenda (1 min)
"Hi [Owner Name], thanks for jumping on. As promised, I want to keep this extremely short and respect your time. 

My goal today is simply to ask a few quick questions about how [Company Name] currently handles incoming web leads, show you the quick 3-minute mockup I put together, and if it makes sense, talk about how we can deploy it. If it’s not a fit, that is completely fine too. Does that sound fair?"

---

## Phase 2: Diagnostic Questions (7 mins)
Ask these questions and **listen 80% of the time**. Take detailed notes.

- "Walk me through what happens when a prospect lands on your site at 8 PM and wants to book an appointment. How do they contact you?"
- "If they fill out your contact form or leave a voicemail, how long does it typically take your office staff to follow up and confirm a slot?"
- "What happens if your staff is busy or out of the office? Do you have a sense of how many prospective clients call or email and never get a response?"
- "If you capture just one extra [Niche] customer per month (e.g. one HVAC install or one dental patient), what is that worth to your business financially?"

---

## Phase 3: The Mockup Reveal (4 mins)
"Based on what you just shared, it sounds like your main bottleneck is that staff spending 3 hours a day playing phone tag to confirm bookings, and you're dropping leads that come in after hours.

Let me share my screen and show you the basic mockup I drafted for [Company Name]."

*(Share screen and demo the chatbot)*

"This chatbot sits in the bottom right corner. When a user lands at 8 PM, they are immediately greeted. The bot answers their top FAQs (insurance, pricing, location) and lets them choose a booking slot that syncs directly with your calendar. 

Instead of filling out a long form, they book in 45 seconds. Your staff gets an automated notification, and the lead is saved.

How does that process compare to how you currently capture prospects?"

---

## Phase 4: Propose the Next Step (3 mins)
"If we wanted to move forward, here is what the setup looks like:

I custom-build the chatbot to match [Company Name]'s exact services and FAQs, connect it to your scheduling calendar, and help you install a simple 1-line script on your site. We can have this live in 7 business days.

The setup investment is a one-time flat fee of $500. After it's live, we handle all maintenance, adjustments, and hosting for a flat $99/month.

If this helps you secure just one new customer a month, it covers the entire annual service cost. 

How would you like to proceed?"
- *If they say yes*: "Great. I will email you a simple 1-page proposal and invoice today. Once that's approved, we start the build."
- *If they need to think*: "No problem at all. I will send you the proposal outline so you have all details in writing. Should we check back next Tuesday?"
