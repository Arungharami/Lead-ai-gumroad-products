# Buyer Instructions — Lead.AI Client Acquisition Command Center

Welcome to the Lead.AI Client Acquisition Command Center. Follow this guide to unlock your files, select your first target niches, and begin executing your 7-day client-finding plan.

---

## Prerequisites

- **Required tools**: A web browser (Google Chrome, Firefox, Safari) and a text reader or markdown editor (VS Code, Obsidian, Notion, or text editor) to view guides.
- **Required accounts**: A free ChatGPT (OpenAI) or Claude (Anthropic) account to run the opportunity audit prompts.
- **Prior knowledge**: Basic understanding of what AI tools can do (chatbots, auto-responses, scheduling integrations) so you can explain them simply.
- **Time required**: 1–2 hours to set up your target list and outreach templates.

---

## Step 1: Download and Unpack Your Files

Once your payment is processed, Gumroad will prompt you to download the zip archive.

1. Download the ZIP file `dist/ai-client-acquisition-command-center-v1.0.0.zip` to your local drive.
2. Unzip the folder to your desktop or project directory.
3. Open `START-HERE.md` inside your text reader/editor. It maps out all 10 custom worksheets, prompt files, call scripts, and tracking spreadsheets included in the package.

*If you do not receive the download email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder. Still missing? Email a.gharami.325@westcliff.edu with the subject line "Download issue — Client Acquisition Center" and your Gumroad order number.*

---

## Step 2: Select Your First 2 Target Niches

Before sending any emails, you need to know who you are targeting and what their specific business problems are.

1. Open `01-ideal-client-profile-worksheet.md`. Read the criteria for choosing high-value niches (high average transaction size, clear operational overhead).
2. Open `02-local-business-niche-list.md`. Review the 15 pre-audited local business niches.
3. Select **2 niches** to start with (for example: Real Estate Agents and Dental Clinics). 

*If successful, you will have a clear idea of which local services have the highest willingness to pay for customer support chatbots and automated lead capturing.*

---

## Step 3: Audit 5 Prospects and Build Your Target Lead List

Next, you will research local businesses in your area and find specific service gaps.

1. Open `04-lead-research-workflow.md` to learn how to search local businesses using Google Maps and Yelp.
2. Build a list of 5 target companies. Locate their websites and decision-maker contact details.
3. Open `03-ai-opportunity-audit-prompts.md`. Copy the "Website Audit Prompt" and paste it into ChatGPT/Claude along with notes about the prospect's public website.
4. Record your findings, contact emails, and audit results in the `09-simple-crm-tracker.csv`.

*If successful, you will have 5 audited leads with identified automation gaps, ready for highly personalized value-dense outreach.*

---

## Step 4: Customize and Send Value-First Outreach

Now you will reach out to decision-makers by highlighting their specific support leaks and proposing simple fixes.

1. Open `05-personalized-outreach-templates.md`. Choose either the cold email template or the LinkedIn DM template.
2. Inject your prospect's specific audit findings (e.g. "noticed your web chat is inactive after 5 PM, which means you are dropping evening leads").
3. Send your outreach messages.
4. If you don't receive an answer in 3 business days, open `06-follow-up-sequence.md` and send follow-up message 1.

*If successful, you will start booking 15-minute diagnostic discovery calls with interested owners. When they book, open `07-discovery-call-script.md` to guide the conversation and `08-ai-automation-proposal-template.md` to structure their setup package.*
