## Your First 10 Minutes

**Goal: Map your workspace files and select your initial target niches.**

1. Unzip `dist/ai-client-acquisition-command-center-v1.0.0.zip` to a new folder on your computer.
2. Open `START-HERE.md` inside your markdown viewer or text editor. Review the structure of the worksheets, prompts, cold emails, call scripts, proposals, and CRM trackers.
3. Open `01-ideal-client-profile-worksheet.md`. Read the three selection criteria (transaction volume, digital leak potential, and willingness to invest).
4. Open `02-local-business-niche-list.md`. Select exactly **2 local niches** that fit these criteria (for example: Real Estate Agents for lead capture chatbots, and Dental Clinics for booking automation).

**Your 10-minute milestone:** You have successfully mapped your workspace and chosen your first two target niches to focus your prospecting activities.

## Your First 1 Hour

**Goal: Research 5 local businesses, audit their assets, and populate your CRM.**

1. Open your web browser and search Google Maps or Yelp for local businesses matching your selected niches (e.g., "Real Estate agencies in [Your City]").
2. Find **5 local businesses** that have active websites but lack interactive chat support, modern lead forms, or rapid FAQ answering.
3. Open `03-ai-opportunity-audit-prompts.md`. Copy the "Website Audit Prompt" and run it in ChatGPT or Claude, pasting in the text or details of the prospect's landing page. Let the model analyze their digital lead capture leaks.
4. Open `09-simple-crm-tracker.csv` in Excel or import it into Notion. Record the business name, website, decision-maker name (if found), contact email, and the 2 main automation opportunities discovered in your audit.

**Your 1-hour milestone:** You have built a highly targeted, pre-audited pipeline of 5 local business prospects with identified lead-capture gaps, logged neatly in your CRM tracker.

---

## Your First Day

**Goal: Customize and send your first value-dense cold outreach emails.**

1. Open `05-personalized-outreach-templates.md`. Review the "Cold Email Audit Pitch" and the "LinkedIn Value Message."
2. Create draft messages for the 5 prospects in your CRM. Replace the brackets with the prospect's company name and the specific gaps discovered during your 1-hour audit (e.g., "I noticed your contact form has 8 required fields, which typically drops conversions by 30% compared to a simple 3-field conversational chatbot").
3. Proofread your drafts to ensure they are polite, value-first, and completely free of hype or income promises.
4. Send your 5 outreach messages and record the "Sent Date" in your CRM tracker. Set a reminder in your calendar to send follow-up message 1 from `06-follow-up-sequence.md` in 3 business days if you don't receive a reply.

**Your day-one milestone:** You have launched your first prospecting campaign by sending 5 highly personalized, audit-backed outreach messages to local businesses.
