## Your first 10 minutes

Print it or keep it open on a second screen. Go to Phase 1 right now and answer this question:

**"My product helps [WHO] do [WHAT] so they can [RESULT]."**

If you can not fill in those three blanks yet, you are not ready to launch — and that is okay. Knowing that saves you from launching something that will not convert.

---

## How to use the checklist

Work through each phase in order. Do not skip to Phase 3 (Gumroad setup) before Phase 1 and 2 are complete. The phases are ordered for a reason: a clear product and product page will outsell a polished Gumroad page with an unclear product every time.
