# Email Capture Plan — Free AI Product Launch Checklist

**Strategy for converting free checklist downloaders into paid buyers.**

---

## How email capture works

When someone downloads a free product on Gumroad, Gumroad automatically captures their email address. You can see all your buyers (including free) in Gumroad's audience section.

You can email your Gumroad audience from Gumroad's built-in email tool (Gumroad Discover → Audience → Email Audience).

---

## 7-day follow-up sequence after free checklist download

Use the prompts in `marketing/email-sequence-prompts.md` to generate the actual email content. Here is the strategy:

**Day 0 (immediate):** Receipt message — friendly, specific, one action to take now.

**Day 1 email (24 hours after download):**
- Subject: "Did you fill in Phase 1?"
- Content: 2–3 sentences asking which phase they are on and offering to answer one question
- CTA: Reply with a question

**Day 3 email:**
- Subject: "The #1 mistake on the launch checklist"
- Content: One specific, useful insight about product launches (related to Phase 4 or 5)
- CTA: "If you want the full product system, here's the $10 playbook: [link]"

**Day 5 email:**
- Subject: "What [specific buyer type] use this for"
- Content: Honest description of a use case (not a testimonial — just a use case description)
- CTA: "Here are the tools that go with this checklist: [product links]"

**Day 7 email:**
- Subject: "The full Lead.AI toolkit"
- Content: Clear, honest introduction to the bundle — what it includes, what it costs, what it is for
- CTA: "$79 bundle → [link when ready]"

---

## Ongoing emails

After the 7-day sequence, send:
- Product update announcements when new versions launch
- New product announcements
- Occasional free tips (from `marketing/weekly-update-prompts.md`)

---

## What NOT to do

- Do not email more than 2–3 times per week
- Do not send discount codes you did not promise
- Do not send "LAST CHANCE" urgency emails for digital products (there is no last chance)
- Do not fake scarcity
- Do not send content that is just a sales pitch — every email must have standalone value

---

## Goal

Convert 10–20% of free checklist downloaders into buyers of the $10 playbook within 30 days.

Convert 2–5% of free checklist downloaders into bundle buyers within 60 days.
