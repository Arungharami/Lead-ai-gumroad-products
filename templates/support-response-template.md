# Support Response Template

**Instructions:** Use these as starting points for support email replies. Always personalize — do not copy-paste robotically. The buyer can tell the difference.

---

## Template 1: Download issue

```
Hi [NAME],

Thanks for reaching out. Let's get your download sorted.

[OPTION A — if they haven't received the email:]
Your download link is sent by Gumroad automatically after purchase. If it is not 
in your inbox, check your spam/junk folder for an email from hello@gumroad.com. 
If it is not there, you can also access your purchases directly at: 
https://app.gumroad.com/library — log in with the email you used to purchase.

[OPTION B — if the link has expired:]
Gumroad download links expire after a set number of uses. You can always access 
your purchases at https://app.gumroad.com/library. If you still can't get in, 
reply here and I will send you a new link directly.

Let me know if that resolves it.

Arun
```

---

## Template 2: Technical question (code/setup)

```
Hi [NAME],

Thanks for the question — this is a common one.

[SPECIFIC ANSWER TO THEIR QUESTION]

If that does not resolve it, try [ALTERNATIVE STEP]. 

Let me know what happens and I will keep helping until it is working.

Arun
```

**Rules for technical responses:**
- Never give a vague answer like "make sure your environment is set up correctly"
- Always give one specific thing to try
- If you do not know the answer, say so and tell them you will find out

## Template 5: Complaint about product quality

```
Hi [NAME],

I am sorry [SPECIFIC ISSUE] was not what you expected. Let me help.

[SPECIFIC RESOLUTION: either fix the issue, explain what they may have missed, 
or offer a refund]

If [RESOLUTION] does not work for you, I am happy to issue a full refund — just 
let me know and I will process it right away.

Arun
```

---

## Response timing rule

Reply within 48 hours on business days. If you need more time (you're researching an answer), send a brief acknowledgment within 24 hours: "Got your question — I am checking on this and will get back to you by [SPECIFIC DATE]."
