# Receipt Message Template

**Instructions:** Copy this to `receipt-message.md` in a new product folder and customize. Paste the final version into Gumroad → Product → "Thank you / Receipt message." Keep under 300 words.

---

Hi — thank you for [purchasing / getting] [PRODUCT NAME].

Your [download / ZIP file / PDF] is attached to this message. [One-sentence description of what to do with it immediately.]

Here is what to do right now (takes [TIME ESTIMATE]):

1. [First action — specific and achievable in under 10 minutes]
2. [Second action — the thing that makes them feel like they made the right purchase]
3. [Optional third action]

[One sentence on what they will have accomplished by doing those 3 things.]

If you [hit an error / have a question / can't find something], email me at a.gharami.325@westcliff.edu with "[PRODUCT SHORT NAME] question" in the subject line. [What to include in the email — e.g., error message, step number.] I respond within 48 hours.

When I [update this product / add new content / release a new version], you will get it free. [How they will be notified — Gumroad email.]

[One closing sentence — personal, specific to the product. Not generic "Thank you for your support."]

[YOUR NAME]  
Lead.AI  
https://github.com/Arungharami/Lead-ai-gumroad-products

---

**Checklist before pasting into Gumroad:**
- [ ] Under 300 words
- [ ] No placeholder [BRACKETS] remaining
- [ ] First action is achievable in 10 minutes
- [ ] Email address is correct
- [ ] Sign-off is personal and specific
