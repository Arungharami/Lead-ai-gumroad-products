# Refund Response Template

**Policy:** Full refund within 7 days of purchase. No conditions required. Honor promptly.

---

## Template 1: Standard refund (buyer requests, within policy window)

```
Hi [NAME],

Absolutely — I will process the refund right away.

[Process the refund in Gumroad: Gumroad → Sales → find the order → Refund]

You should see the refund in your account within 5–7 business days depending on 
your payment method.

If you are open to sharing what did not work for you, I would genuinely appreciate 
the feedback — it helps me improve. But no pressure at all.

Arun
```

---

## Template 2: Refund with feedback opportunity

```
Hi [NAME],

No problem — refund is on its way. You should see it within 5–7 business days.

One thing I would genuinely find helpful: was there a specific thing in the product 
that did not match what you expected? Even a one-sentence answer helps me improve it.

If the product was not the right fit for where you are right now, the [OTHER PRODUCT 
AT LOWER PRICE] might be closer to what you need: [LINK]. But absolutely no pressure 
— just wanted to make sure you are not left without a useful resource.

Arun
```

---

## Template 3: Refund request after 7 days (still honor if reasonable)

```
Hi [NAME],

Our policy covers refunds within 7 days, but I understand this slipped past that 
window. Let me process it anyway.

[Process the refund]

Refund is on its way — 5–7 business days.

Arun
```

**Note:** For digital products, honoring late refund requests costs you one sale but saves your reputation. It is almost always worth it.

---

## Template 4: Refund request that might be fixable

```
Hi [NAME],

I can absolutely process a refund if that is what you want — no issues at all.

Before I do, I want to make sure the problem was not something I can quickly resolve. 
[DESCRIBE THE ISSUE THEY MENTIONED AND A POSSIBLE SOLUTION].

If [SOLUTION] does not work or you would just prefer the refund, reply and I will 
process it immediately.

Arun
```

---

## Processing a refund on Gumroad

1. Go to Gumroad → Analytics → Sales
2. Find the buyer's order
3. Click "Refund"
4. Gumroad processes the refund automatically

---

## Rule: never make a refund request difficult

Do not:
- Ask why they want a refund (you can invite feedback, but do not require it)
- Add conditions or delays
- Make them feel guilty
- Offer partial refunds instead of full ones

A fast, gracious refund is marketing. A difficult refund process is the opposite.
